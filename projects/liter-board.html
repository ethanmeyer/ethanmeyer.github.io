Liter Board

Technologies Used: Flutter | Dart

May 2021

This project was done solo by me for a hackathon hosted by the Seattle Pacific University Developer Club called FalconHax. This app is run using the Flutter platform made by Google. This app would run on your phone to both track how much water your are drinking and compete with your friends to see who can meet their goals of healthy water consumtion at the same time.
