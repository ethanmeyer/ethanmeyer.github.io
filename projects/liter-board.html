Liter Board

Technologies Used: Flutter | Dart

May 2021

I completed this solo project as an entry for the FalconHax hackathon hosted by the Seattle Pacific University Developers Club. The app is run using Flutter, an open-source framework made by Google. Liter Board allows you to use your phone to track your water intake while competing with your friends to see who’s staying hydrated. The app’s goal is to promote healthy water consumption through friendly competition. You can find a video demoing the project here.
