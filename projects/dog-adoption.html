Dog Adoption App

Technologies Used: C++ | QT

March 2020

This desktop application was a group project in my Application Programming course. We used QT to make the application. Our idea behind the application was an application which would match breeds of dogs to owners after the owner takes a quiz on their lifestyle. This solved the problem of new dog owners not picking a breed of dog which works for their lifestyle, like if someone who is not active adopted a husky and did not properly care for the very active husky.
